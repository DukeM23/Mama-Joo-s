ABOUT US

We love hot sauces as much as the next guy. We love hotsauces so much that we went out of our way to make our own hotsauces! Inspired by Korean flavours and our experiences in the food & service industry, we carefully craft micro-batched artisan hot-sauces that are made and bottled by hand. We want to share our exepriences from our humbles beginnings working in kitchens to smelling food cooked by our Mom once we get home.


Due to the nature of production for our hot-sauces, there will always be a variations from one bottle to another.This is where we coined our motto "No Two Batches Alike!"
