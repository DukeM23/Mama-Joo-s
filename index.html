"No Two Batches Alike!"
- Mama Joo

"Using recipes inspired by his Korean background, as well as new and noteworthy flavours, founder Daniel Joo is stepping the hot sauce trade up a notch.''

- Alyssa Shull, Taste Toronto

The sauces are made in micro-batches, which speaks to their slogan, "No two batches alike!!" meaning the sauces can vary intensity, but they are all packing a fiery punch. A few of Joo's favourites include the 'O.G.' hot sauce and the 'Raging Relish' hot relish.
